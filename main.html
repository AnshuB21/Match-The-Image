Memory Game

Score: 0

How to Play:

1. Choose a game mode: 8, 10, or 12 images.
2. Match each image with its identical pair.
3. Complete the game within the allotted time:
 • 8 images: 120 seconds
 • 10 images: 150 seconds
 • 12 images: 180 seconds
4. Click two cards to reveal them, and wait for unmatched cards to reset.
5. Time runs out? Refresh the page to restart.
